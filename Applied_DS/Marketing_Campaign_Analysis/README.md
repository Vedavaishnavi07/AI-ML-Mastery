# Marketing Campaign Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes a bank marketing campaign to identify which customer segments are more likely to subscribe to a term deposit.

The analysis includes:

- Data Cleaning
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Customer Segmentation
- Chi-Square Hypothesis Test
- Independent t-Test
- Data Visualization
- Business Insights

---

## Dataset

Bank Marketing Dataset

Rows: 11,162

Columns: 17

Target Variable:

deposit

## Statistical Tests

### Chi-Square Test

Purpose:

Determine whether contact method affects customer subscription.

Result:

A statistically significant relationship was found.

---

### Independent t-Test

Purpose:

Compare average account balances between customers who subscribed and those who did not.

Result:

Customers who subscribed had significantly different account balances.

---

## Business Insights

- Cellular contact performs best.
- Higher account balance increases subscription likelihood.
- Management and technician customers represent major customer groups.
- Campaign achieved nearly 47% subscriptions.
- Contact strategy plays a significant role in campaign success.
